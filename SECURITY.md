# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

We are committed to ensuring the security of the Polar project. We will provide security updates for
the latest major version of Polar.

| Version | Supported          |
| ------- | ------------------ |
| 1.x.x   | :white_check_mark: |

## Reporting a Vulnerability

The Polar team and community take all security vulnerabilities seriously. Thank you for your efforts
to responsibly disclose your findings.

To report a security vulnerability, please send an email to **usbertibox@gmail.com**.

Please include the following details with your report:

- A clear and descriptive title.
- A detailed description of the vulnerability, including how to reproduce it.
- The version of Polar you're using.
- Any proof-of-concept code or screenshots that might help us understand the issue.

We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours and will send you a more detailed response within 72
hours indicating the next steps in handling your report. After the initial reply to your report, the
security team will keep you informed of the progress towards a fix and full announcement, and may
ask for additional information or guidance.

We request that you do not disclose the vulnerability publicly until we have had a chance to address
it. We will do our best to work with you to make sure that we can release a fix as quickly as
possible.

We will credit you for your discovery in the release notes.
